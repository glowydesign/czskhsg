Our Story

Two Flags, One Community

The Czech & Slovak Union brings together Czech and Slovak students at the University of St. Gallen, along with everyone who feels a connection to our languages, culture, and traditions.

We organize social and cultural events throughout the year, welcome new students with a helping hand, and keep a little piece of home close, even far away from it.
