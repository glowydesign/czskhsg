For Sponsors & Donors

As a young association, sponsorships and donations directly fund our events, our first merchandise drop, and our ability to welcome new students properly.

Every Contribution Helps

Whether you're a company interested in sponsoring an event, an alumna or alumnus who wants to give back, or simply someone who believes in what we're building, we are happy for any donation, big or small.

If you'd like to donate or discuss a sponsorship, please contact us directly and we'll get back to you promptly.
