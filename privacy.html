Privacy Policy

How the Czech & Slovak Union handles your data: straightforward and transparent.

Last updated: 2026

1. Who We Are

The Czech & Slovak Union is a non-profit student association at the University of St. Gallen (HSG), Switzerland. You can reach us at placeholder@email.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us, for example when you fill out our contact form, our merchandise interest form, or ask to be added to our WhatsApp or Instagram community (name, email address, and any message content you choose to share). We do not use tracking cookies, analytics tools, or advertising networks on this website.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data submitted via our forms is used solely to respond to your inquiry, process merchandise interest, or add you to our community channels, and to contact you about the Union's activities and events. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Third-Party Form Processing

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service. When you submit the contact form, your data is transmitted to and briefly processed by Formspree in order to deliver your message to us. Please refer to Formspree's own privacy policy for details on their processing.

5. Data Storage & Security

Submitted form data is received via email and stored securely by the Board. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect anonymised server logs as part of its standard infrastructure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you, including removal from our WhatsApp or Instagram community. To exercise these rights, contact us at placeholder@email.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. External Links

This website contains links to external sites (Instagram, Formspree, the University of St. Gallen). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please email us at placeholder@email.com or reach out via Instagram @placeholder.
